About Me

I am a backend oriented software engineer who is passionate about data. The process of extracting data from the world and organizing it to provide value back to the world is fascinating to me. Another aspect of technology that excites me is creating platforms that automate processes to create value through greater efficiency. Currently, I am working with health claims data to reduce prescription medication costs to patients. I am always looking for new opportunities to expand my skills and use technology to create good in the world.

My Projects

MyClimatePal is an app that allows users to input the food they eat and track the environmental impact of their food choices. It is a full-stack app created with MongoDB, Express.js, ReactJS and Node.js.

FitJournal is a workout tracker that lets you log your sets and reps of exercises you do. It is made with Django, ReactJS + Redux, PostgreSQL. It is deployed and scaled on Amazon Web Services with the use of Elastic Beanstalk.
